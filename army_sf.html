Para(Special Forces), also known as Para SF, is the special operations unit of the Indian Army. It is attached to the Parachute Regiment. The unit specializes in various roles including hostage rescue, counter-terrorism, unconventional warfare, special reconnaissance, foreign internal defense, counter-proliferation, counter-insurgency, seek and destroy and personnel recovery.

Also known as "Red Devils"

Insignia

  • Maroon Beret
  • Balidan Badge

Formed on 1 July 1966.
